Tokens – how Claude reads everything you give it

Claude doesn't read your messages the way you do. Instead, it converts everything into tokensTokenA small chunk of text, roughly one word or word-fragment. The model reads and generates text one token at a time. Everything (text, code, images) gets converted to tokens.Think of tokens as the model's alphabet. – small chunks of text, roughly one word or word-fragment each. Every piece of input you send (your question, any documents, any context) and every word Claude writes back is counted in tokens. Understanding this is the foundation for making sense of how Claude's memory works, which comes next.

Example sentence

"Summarize the Q3 client engagement report and highlight key risks"
